Your Donation Creates Lasting Impact

At HopeBridge Foundation, we are committed to ensuring that every rupee you donate reaches those who need it most. With 95% of all donations directed straight to our program activities, you can be confident that your contribution is creating real, measurable change.

We are registered under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, and hold 80G and 12A certifications. All donations are tax-exempt under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We provide detailed receipts and annual impact reports to all our donors.
